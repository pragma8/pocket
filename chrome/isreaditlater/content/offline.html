Using Pocket Offline

Pocket can download your pages so they are available offline. There are two different views you can choose to download:

Web View

The entire page as you would normally see in your web browser. This includes all images and therefore can take longer to download.

Text View

Just the article text, formatted for easy reading. No images are included which makes downloading faster.

How to Download Content:

Open your reading list and click the download button (). Select the views you wish to download (see descriptions above) and click 'Download Reading List'.

Your list will begin downloading in the background so you can do something else while you wait. When downloading is completed, all of your items will be available offline.

How to View Offline Content:

This is all handled for you automatically. If you have no internet connection and attempt to open a page from your list, Pocket will automatically serve you the offline version.
